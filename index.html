Explore

For Kids

  • Looking to learn more about science?
  • Learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing.
  • Travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth.
  • Join our Young Stars club and experiment.
  • Join our Holiday club.
  • Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds.
  • Make sure you come ready to learn and explore!
Contact us for information

For Teachers

  • As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring.
  • We can give your students guided tours of the museum.
  • We can teach them in our learning laboratory.
  • We can provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.
Contact us for information

For Researchers

  • Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics?
  • We offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute towards them.
  • We have online records, laboratory space, and a working relationship with a number of universities around the country.
Contact us for information
